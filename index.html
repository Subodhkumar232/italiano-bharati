💬 A Beginner Conversation

Marco (Teacher)
Ciao! Come ti chiami? Hello! What is your name?
Priya (Student)
Ciao! Mi chiamo Priya. Come stai? Hello! My name is Priya. How are you?
Marco
Sto bene, grazie! E tu? I am fine, thank you! And you?
Priya
Anch'io sto bene, grazie! I am also fine, thank you!
Marco
Benissimo! Da dove vieni? Very good! Where are you from?
Priya
Vengo dall'India. E tu? I come from India. And you?
✅ This is a real A1-level conversation you'll master in Week 2!
